About

I'm a specialist in molecular genetics and genetic engineering in plant, for example my work in investigates the role of transporters and ion channels in the plant and its adaptation to abiotic stresses: K+ deficiency.
The plants studied are mainly cereals (rice , barley, wheat ), rice being selected as a model species.
Targeted transport systems mainly belong to the family of K+ and / or Na+ HKT transporters, and families of K+ carriers .
