About

Fábia Berlatto is a PhD student in sociology at UFPR/Brazil. She is now doing an internship at the Centre for Sociological Research on Law and Penal Institutions (CNRS/University of Versailles/Ministry of Justice - France). She is affiliated with two research centers in Brazil: Center for study in public security and human rights and the Observatory of social and political elites of Brazil. Her interests are in the questions of governance of security, social and political elites.
