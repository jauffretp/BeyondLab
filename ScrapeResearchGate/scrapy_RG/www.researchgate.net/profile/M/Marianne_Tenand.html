About

I am a PhD Candidate at PSE. My fields of research include health economics, social policies, long-term care and microeconometrics.
I am currently working on the impacts of long-term care schemes on the utilization of formal and informal care. I am also interested in the health expenditures of the disabled elderly.
My research uses French data but I am much interested in learning more about the institutional organisations of LTC and healthcare in other countries.
