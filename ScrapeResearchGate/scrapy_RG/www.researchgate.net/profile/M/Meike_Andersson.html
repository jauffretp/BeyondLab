About

Scientist with >10 yrs experience in international agricultural research and development. PhD Agricultural Sciences from Stuttgart-Hohenheim University, MSc Biology from Hamburg University. Before joining HarvestPlus I was Markets Project Manager for Ecoagriculture Partners, improving conservation outcomes of agricultural production systems. Experience in R4D, implementation of research strategies and methods, coordination of national and international projects in Latin America, Africa and Asia.
