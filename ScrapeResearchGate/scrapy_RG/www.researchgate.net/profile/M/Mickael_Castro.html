About

Dr. Mickaël CASTRO is currently lecturer at University of South Brittany, France since 2009. He obtained his PhD from the University of Saint-Etienne in 2004, studying the morphology and the rheological behaviour of co-continuous immiscible polymer blends. During his post-doctorate at the University of Minnesota, he studied the rheology of concentrated surfactant systems. Then he joined the Materials Engineering Laboratory of Brittany in the Smart Plastics Group in 2006.
