About

Medical Doctor, specialist in Geriatrics and Gerontology, PhD in Preventive Medicine in Older Persons. Currently, Professor at the University of Toulouse (France).

Editor-in-Chief of the J Frailty Aging, and Associate Editor of J Gerontol Med Sci. Member of the Editorial Board of several journals, including JAMDA and J Nutr Health Aging.

Research interests: Frailty, Aging, Physical disability, Physical Performance, Gait speed, Body composition, Sarcopenia, Comprehensive Geriatric Assessment.
