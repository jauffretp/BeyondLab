About

Mohamed Benbouzid is a Professor of Electrical Engineering at the University of Brest (France). He is also a Distinguished Professor at the Shanghai Maritime University (China). His main research interests include analysis, design, fault detection/diagnosis, and control of electric machines, variable-speed drives for traction, propulsion, and renewable energy applications.
Prof. Benbouzid is the Editor-in-Chief of the IRECON Journal and an Associate Editor of the IEEE TEC, TIE, TSTE, and TVT.
