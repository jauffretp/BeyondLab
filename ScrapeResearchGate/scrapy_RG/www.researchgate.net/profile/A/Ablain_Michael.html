About

I'm responsible of the altimetry validation activities at CLS (Toulouse, France). This work aims at analyzing the performances of all the altimeter missions especially over ocean. My studies allows us to better know the error budget of altimeter mission at different spatial and temporal scales, in particular for the global and regional Mean Sea Level evolution.
