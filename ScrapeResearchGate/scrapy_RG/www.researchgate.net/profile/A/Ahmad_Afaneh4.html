About

Ahmad is a creative and enthusiastic architect and urban planner who is particularly interested in Smart City solutions, technologies and GIS for urban planning.A Master's Degree in urban and regional planning with a concentration on urban planning for small towns and cities. Currently, a PhD candidate in the area of Civil and Geo-Environment, in particular of " Development of GIS-Based urban information for Sustainable, resilient and Smart City.
