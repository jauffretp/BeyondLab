About

I am interested in studying contaminant-related stress in organisms across neurochemical pathways and genetic approaches. I’m working on neuroendocrine targets of chemicals acting as endocrine disruptors in different species, especially fish. We aim to develop new and predictive strategies based on developing and applying in vitro toxicity assays to predict cellular level effects that could be extrapolated to effects on individuals.
