About

Between 2014 and 2016 I worked on applied micro-economic research for the World Bank and the International Food Policy Research Institute. Some of the topics I touched upon are shared prosperity, financial inclusion, poverty, nutrition and resilience to climate change.
In September 2016 I joined the Paris School of Economics with the plan of studying the economic consequences of European immigration policies, aiming to continue with a PhD thesis on the same topic.
