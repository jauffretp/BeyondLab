About

FORGREENE: TROPICAL FORESTS GREENESS AND CLOUDINESS IN CENTRAL AFRICA: present conditions and future evolution
FORGREENE will rely on a consortium from 8 French research centers, the French company TRANSVALOR expert in solar irradiation, and researchers from Belgium, Cameroon, Gabon, the Central African, the Congo and the Congo Democratic Republics; setup an original database with novel in situ measurements of ecoclimatic parameters; explore original assumptions and develop innovative analyses.
