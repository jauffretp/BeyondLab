About

As a plant ecologist, the objectives of my research are mainly on assembly rules and species-coexistence in plant communities. I'am particularly interested in long-term resilience of herbaceous plant communities after major disturbance. I'm involved in many restoration projects of Mediterranean rangelands by the use of ecological engineering involving the use of ecosystem engineers (nurse species, ants, etc.).
