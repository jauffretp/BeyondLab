About

I am particularly interested in studying the chromatin organisation and dynamics, especially how the levels of chromatin compaction are regulated in vivo using advanced fluorescence microscopy approaches such as FLIM-FRET. I'm actually focusing my research on epigenetic/genomic imprinting field in pluripotent stem cells.
