About

I am currently post-doctoral fellow, at the Laboratoire Informatique de Nantes Atlantique of the University of Nantes.
I received my PhD in 2013 from the University of Tours.
My research topic is mainly focused on the field of big data, in particular: data mining algorithms, hadoop systems, log mining, log summaries, recommendation systems and OLAP.
