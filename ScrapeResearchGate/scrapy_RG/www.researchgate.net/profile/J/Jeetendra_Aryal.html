About

I am a Nepalese citizen, working as a Climate Economist in CIMMYT, Mexico now. I joined CIMMYT in 2012 and worked in South Asia until September 15, 2016. I have worked under several projects including CCAFS, CSISA, SRFSI and Adoption and Impact of laser land leveling in India. My works focus on climate change (CC) and its impact on agriculture, CC adaptation and mitigation, adaptation plan of action, climate smart agriculture, gender and technology adoption, and caste-based discrimination.
