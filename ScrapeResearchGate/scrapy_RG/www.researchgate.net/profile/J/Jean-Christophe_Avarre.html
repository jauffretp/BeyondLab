About

Jean-Christophe Avarre is researcher at the Institute of Evolutionary Sciences of Montpellier (ISEM), and is heading a team called DIVA (Fish Diversity and Aquaculture). He is a molecular biologist with a strong experience in molecular physiology and microbial diagnostics. He has been working for a biotech company where he participated in the conception and validation of innovative solutions for genomics and diagnostics applications.
