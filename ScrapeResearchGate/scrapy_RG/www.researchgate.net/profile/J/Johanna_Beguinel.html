About

My PhD work focuses on the study of the fiber/matrix interface in new continuous fiber thermoplastic composites. The designed composite material combines an outsider acrylic matrix to a new fabric impregnation technique. The micro-scale is related to the macro-scale by mechanical testing and physico-chemical characterizations at both scales. The PhD is funded by Chomarat, and run in close collaboration with Arkema.
