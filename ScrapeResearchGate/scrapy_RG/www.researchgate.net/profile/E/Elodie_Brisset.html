About

Increasing demographic pressures and hazards related to climate change require understanding landscape sensitivity to erosion. Retrospective analysis of environmental trajectories from lake sediments and multidisciplinary proxies (geomorphology, geochemistry, palynology), enable quantification of erosion over millennia. It allows to study ecosystem robustness, elasticity and regime shift to perturbations.

Case study: Alps, Bolivia, Chile, Kirghizistan, Iran
