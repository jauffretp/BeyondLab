About

I am currently research scientist Department of Metallic Materials and Structure.

At the moment, I am mainly focused on the development of Robust domain decomposition methods for complex solid mechanics computations.

I worked previously on multibody dynamics and predictive model for rolling bearing failure. The model use multibody dynamic formalism, finite element structural element and specific rolling contact model (EHD, Kalker non linear creep theory).
