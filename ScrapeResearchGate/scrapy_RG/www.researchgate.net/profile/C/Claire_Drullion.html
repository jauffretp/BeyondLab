About

After working on senescence as a barriere against leukemogenesis in mouse T-ALL model, I work know as a research assistance in B-ALL disease.
One of my projects is to identify protein interaction with a protein tumor suppressor ETV6. I also participate to the validation of genetic variants in cell line system and to a starting project on Long non coding RNA.
