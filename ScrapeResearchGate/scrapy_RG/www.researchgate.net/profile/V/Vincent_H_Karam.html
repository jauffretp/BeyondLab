About

Vincent H Karam have over 20 years’ experience in clinical studies. He undertook the challenge of developing the European Liver Transplant Registry since 1995. Data from the ELTR has formed the foundation for the development of risk models for LT outcomes. He is continuously seeking innovative projects. The ELTR group in LinkedIn offers a platform to the sharing of innovative experiences with the goal of increasing access to the LT and improvement of results.
