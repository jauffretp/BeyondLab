About

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. on wireless communications for 5G in SCEE research team, at CentraleSupelec, Rennes, France.
Before that, I obtained the Engineering Degree and M. Sc in wireless communications, embedded electronics and networks, from Supélec (now CentraleSupélec) in Rennes, France.

My research activities focus on designing a new Physical Layer suitable for 5G communications, such as D2D, M2M or IoT communications.
