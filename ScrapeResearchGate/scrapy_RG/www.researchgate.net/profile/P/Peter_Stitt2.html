About

I've recently obtained my PhD and am currently working on the BiLi project at LIMSI in France. My current work is on numerical simulation of HRTFs. I have a strong interest in perceptual evaluation (and corresponding perceptual models) of headphone- and loudspeaker-based spatial audio systems.
