About

Ludivine is an Assistant Professor in HRM at Grenoble Ecole de Management. His research is oriented around emerging management & HRM tools in clusters. Her research interests are also focused on the collaboration between employees working in clusters, and the impact of rewards and variable working times on employee engagement in R&D collaborative projects.
