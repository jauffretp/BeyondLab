About

My main research activity is concerned with studying the structure, dynamics and transport properties of high temperature materials by using various techniques and particularly synchrotron and neutron techniques.
From 2004 to 2014, I was the group leader of the "Synchrotron Radiation and Neutron Research" Group at CNRS-CEMHTI in Orleans (France).
