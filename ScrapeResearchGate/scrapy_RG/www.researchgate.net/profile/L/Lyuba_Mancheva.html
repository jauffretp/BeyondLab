About

I have real passion for computer science and its application in cognitive science for a better understanding of human cognition.

During my PhD thesis I have to design a computational model of the cognitive processes that occur during reading a narrative text. The main goal is to model and simulate the eye movement control and attention as well as the integration and the construction of the meaning of the text that occur during a reading task.
