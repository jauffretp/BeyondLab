About

We are submitting a protocol to validate functional evaluation of "supraspinatus tendon pathologies" under current treatment using a SMS track system. Objectives : 1) validation of the reliability of the systeme compared to the traditional evaluation methods, 2) compare evolution under different treatments (either functionnal or surgical), 3) study and compare the evolution among the four groups (impingement, tendinopathy, partial cull thickness rupture, complete tear of the supraspinatus.
