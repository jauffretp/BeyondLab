About

I develop bottom-up approaches for knowledge acquisition and modelling, natural language processing and terminology-based knowledge engineering. My work benefits from collaborations with linguists and researchers in human factors. Contributions include methods and platforms for ontology engineering from text, tools for pattern-based semantic relation extraction, and knowledge representation to add lexical data to ontologies, with applications in semantic annotation and information retrieval.
