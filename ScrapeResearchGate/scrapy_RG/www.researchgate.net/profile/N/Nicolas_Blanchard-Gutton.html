About

Graduated in endocrinology and cellular interactions, I have previously held a position as research associate in Molecular Medicine Lab of at RCSI. At this role, I developed my skills in molecular biology, microscopic imaging, and deepen my knowledge in cell biology.
Then, I integrated as engineer, the Neurobiology Lab' at ENVA. At this position, I deepened my skills in the field of cell biology and acquired a solid knowledge in histology.
