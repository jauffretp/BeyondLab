About

PhD in Public Health : Epidemiology and Medical data science from the Pierre and Marie Curie University (P6). Operational director of the National database for Rare Diseases, and member of the LIMICS lab (U1142). Strong background in medical data warehouses, semantics, data quality and interoperability in healthcare generated data reuse for public health (epidemiology and surveillance systems). Co-editor of the clinical research informatics section of the IMIA yearbook of medical informatics.
