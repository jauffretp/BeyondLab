About

I am interested in how neurogenesis and morphogenesis steps are coordinated during the olfactory placode development in the zebrafish embryo. In parallel, I am also investigating the origin of the olfactory cell heterogeneity. I'm combining long-term live imaging and transcriptional regulation analysis to unmask these processes.
